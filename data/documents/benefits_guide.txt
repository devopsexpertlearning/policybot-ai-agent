1. ELIGIBILITY AND ENROLLMENT
================================================================================

1.1 Eligibility
Full-time employees (30+ hours/week) are eligible for benefits on the first day
of the month following 30 days of employment.

Part-time employees (20-29 hours/week) are eligible for selected benefits.

1.2 Enrollment Periods
- New Hire: 30 days from hire date
- Annual Open Enrollment: November 1-30 for coverage beginning January 1
- Qualifying Life Event: 30 days from event date

1.3 Qualifying Life Events
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of child
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in employment status
- Death of dependent

1.4 Who You Can Cover
- Spouse or domestic partner
- Dependent children up to age 26
- Disabled dependents over 26 (with approval)

2. HEALTH INSURANCE
================================================================================

TechCorp offers three medical plan options through BlueCross BlueShield.

2.1 Platinum PPO Plan
Best for: Employees who use healthcare frequently

Features:
- Low deductible: $500 individual / $1,000 family
- Low out-of-pocket max: $2,000 individual / $4,000 family
- 90% coinsurance after deductible
- $20 copay for primary care visits
- $40 copay for specialist visits
- Nationwide PPO network

Monthly Premium:
- Employee Only: $50
- Employee + Spouse: $150
- Employee + Child(ren): $120
- Family: $200

2.2 Gold PPO Plan (Most Popular)
Best for: Employees who want balanced coverage and cost

Features:
- Moderate deductible: $1,500 individual / $3,000 family
- Out-of-pocket max: $4,000 individual / $8,000 family
- 80% coinsurance after deductible
- $30 copay for primary care visits
- $50 copay for specialist visits
- Nationwide PPO network

Monthly Premium:
- Employee Only: $25
- Employee + Spouse: $100
- Employee + Child(ren): $80
- Family: $140

2.3 Silver High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) with HSA
Best for: Healthy employees who want to save with an HSA

Features:
- High deductible: $3,000 individual / $6,000 family
- Out-of-pocket max: $6,000 individual / $12,000 family
- 80% coinsurance after deductible
- HSA-compatible (company contributes to your HSA!)
- Preventive care covered 100% (no deductible)

Monthly Premium:
- Employee Only: $0 (Company-paid!)
- Employee + Spouse: $50
- Employee + Child(ren): $40
- Family: $90

Company HSA Contribution (HDHP only):
- Employee Only: $1,000/year
- Family: $2,000/year

2.4 All Plans Include
- Preventive care at 100% (no copay or deductible)
- Prescription drug coverage
- Mental health and substance abuse treatment
- Maternity and newborn care
- Emergency and urgent care
- Hospitalization and surgery
- Telemedicine through MDLive ($0 copay!)

5. LIFE AND AD&D INSURANCE
================================================================================

5.1 Basic Life and AD&D
Company-provided at no cost to employees

Coverage Amount: 2x annual salary (up to $500,000)
Reduction: Coverage reduces to 65% at age 65, 50% at age 70

5.2 Supplemental Life Insurance
You can purchase additional coverage:
- Employee: Up to 5x salary (max $1,000,000)
- Spouse: Up to $250,000
- Child(ren): $10,000 per child

Cost: Based on age and coverage amount
Guarantee Issue: Up to 3x salary without medical underwriting (new hires only)

5.3 Beneficiaries
Designate your beneficiaries online through the Benefits Portal.
Update after major life events.

================================================================================
6. DISABILITY INSURANCE
================================================================================

6.1 Short-Term Disability (STD)
Company-provided at no cost

Benefit: 60% of weekly earnings (up to $2,000/week)
Waiting Period: 7 days for illness, 0 days for accident
Maximum Duration: 13 weeks

6.2 Long-Term Disability (LTD)
Company-provided at no cost

Benefit: 60% of monthly earnings (up to $10,000/month)
Waiting Period: 90 days
Maximum Duration: To age 65 or Social Security retirement age

Benefits are taxable since company pays premium.

6.3 Voluntary LTD Buy-Up
Purchase additional coverage with after-tax dollars to receive tax-free benefits.

7. RETIREMENT SAVINGS (401K)
================================================================================

7.1 401(k) Plan
Administered by Fidelity Investments

Eligibility: Immediate (first day of employment)
Enrollment: Automatic at 3% (can opt out or change)

7.2 Employee Contributions
- Contribute up to IRS maximum ($23,000 for 2026)
- Catch-up contributions if age 50+: Additional $7,500
- Roth 401(k) option available

7.3 Company Match
TechCorp matches dollar-for-dollar up to 6% of your salary!

Example: If you earn $100,000 and contribute 6% ($6,000), TechCorp
contributes another $6,000 - that's free money!

Vesting Schedule:
- Company match vests immediately (100% vested from day one)

7.4 Investment Options
- 20+ investment funds including target-date funds
- Self-directed brokerage option
- Financial planning tools and resources
- Access to Fidelity advisors

7.5 Loans and Withdrawals
- Loans available up to 50% of vested account balance
- Hardship withdrawals permitted for qualified expenses
- Consult plan documents or Fidelity for details

8. HEALTH SAVINGS ACCOUNT (HSA) AND FSA
================================================================================

8.1 Health Savings Account (HSA)
Available only with Silver HDHP plan

2026 Contribution Limits:
- Individual: $4,150
- Family: $8,300
- Age 55+ Catch-up: Additional $1,000

Company Contribution:
- Individual: $1,000
- Family: $2,000

Benefits:
- Triple tax advantage (contribute pre-tax, grow tax-free, withdraw tax-free
  for medical expenses)
- Funds roll over year to year (no "use it or lose it")
- Portable (you keep it if you leave)
- Invest unused balance for retirement
- Use for qualified medical expenses

8.2 Flexible Spending Account (FSA)
Available with Platinum and Gold PPO plans

Healthcare FSA:
- Contribute up to $3,200 pre-tax
- Use for eligible medical/dental expenses
- Use-it-or-lose-it: $640 can roll over to next year

Dependent Care FSA:
- Contribute up to $5,000 pre-tax
- Use for childcare or elder care expenses
- Must be used by year-end (no rollover)
