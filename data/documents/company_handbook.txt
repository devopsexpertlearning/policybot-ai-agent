COMPANY EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK
Version 2.0 - January 2026

================================================================================
WELCOME TO OUR COMPANY
================================================================================

Welcome to TechCorp Solutions! This handbook outlines our company policies,
culture, and guidelines for all employees. Please read it carefully and refer
to it as needed throughout your employment.

================================================================================
1. COMPANY CULTURE AND VALUES
================================================================================

Our Core Values:
- Innovation: We continuously seek better ways to solve problems
- Integrity: We act honestly and ethically in all situations
- Collaboration: We work together to achieve our goals
- Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do
- Diversity: We celebrate and embrace differences

Work Environment:
TechCorp maintains a professional, inclusive, and respectful workplace. We
expect all employees to contribute positively to our culture.

================================================================================
2. EMPLOYMENT POLICIES
================================================================================

2.1 Equal Employment Opportunity
TechCorp is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on
race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other
protected characteristic.

2.2 Work Hours
- Standard work hours: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday
- Total work week: 40 hours
- Flexible work arrangements available upon manager approval
- Remote work options available for eligible positions

2.3 Attendance and Punctuality
Employees are expected to arrive on time and maintain regular attendance.
If you will be absent or late, notify your supervisor as soon as possible.

2.4 Dress Code
Business casual attire is standard. Dress should be professional and appropriate
for client meetings. Casual Fridays allow for jeans and company t-shirts.

================================================================================
3. COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS
================================================================================

3.1 Salary
- Salaries are paid bi-weekly via direct deposit
- Performance reviews conducted annually
- Merit increases based on performance and market conditions

3.2 Overtime
Non-exempt employees are eligible for overtime pay at 1.5x their regular rate
for hours worked over 40 in a workweek.

3.3 Benefits Overview
See the Benefits Guide for detailed information on:
- Health insurance
- Dental and vision coverage
- 401(k) retirement plan with company match
- Life insurance
- Disability coverage
- Employee assistance program (EAP)

================================================================================
4. TIME OFF POLICIES
================================================================================

4.1 Paid Time Off (PTO)
- Years 1-3: 15 days per year
- Years 4-7: 20 days per year
- Years 8+: 25 days per year
- PTO accrues monthly
- Maximum accrual: 1.5x annual allocation

4.2 Holidays
TechCorp observes 10 paid holidays annually:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas Eve
- Christmas Day
- New Year's Eve

4.3 Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 days of sick leave annually, separate from PTO.
Sick leave can be used for personal illness or caring for immediate family.

4.4 Parental Leave
- Primary caregiver: 12 weeks paid leave
- Secondary caregiver: 4 weeks paid leave
- May be extended with unpaid FMLA leave

================================================================================
5. WORKPLACE CONDUCT
================================================================================

5.1 Professional Behavior
All employees must conduct themselves professionally and treat colleagues,
clients, and partners with respect.

5.2 Confidentiality
Employees must protect confidential company information and client data.
Non-disclosure agreements must be honored at all times.

5.3 Conflicts of Interest
Employees must disclose any potential conflicts of interest to their manager
and HR department.

5.4 Social Media
When posting about work or the company on social media, employees should:
- Not share confidential information
- Be respectful and professional
- Make clear that views are personal, not company positions

================================================================================6. PERFORMANCE AND DEVELOPMENT
================================================================================

6.1 Performance Reviews
Annual performance reviews are conducted each January. Reviews include:
- Goal setting and achievement assessment
- Competency evaluation
- Development planning
- Compensation review

6.2 Training and Development
TechCorp invests in employee growth through:
- Annual professional development budget: $2,000 per employee
- In-house training programs
- Conference attendance opportunities
- Tuition reimbursement for approved courses

================================================================================
7. WORKPLACE SAFETY AND SECURITY
================================================================================

7.1 Safety First
Report all safety hazards, injuries, or security concerns immediately.

7.2 Emergency Procedures
Familiarize yourself with emergency exits and procedures. Participate in
all emergency drills.

7.3 Building Access
Use your employee badge for building access. Never allow unauthorized persons
to enter secure areas.

================================================================================
8. TECHNOLOGY USE
================================================================================

8.1 Equipment
Company-provided equipment should be used primarily for business purposes.
Limited personal use is acceptable if it doesn't interfere with work.

8.2 Data Security
Employees must follow all IT security policies. See IT Policy document for
detailed guidelines.

8.3 Internet and Email
Use company internet and email responsibly. All communications may be monitored
and are company property.

================================================================================
9. SEPARATION FROM EMPLOYMENT
================================================================================

9.1 Resignation
Provide at least two weeks' written notice. Exit interviews are conducted
for all departing employees.

9.2 Final Paycheck
Final paychecks include all earned wages and accrued, unused PTO.

9.3 Return of Property
All company property must be returned on or before the last day of employment.

================================================================================
10. ACKNOWLEDGMENT
================================================================================

All employees must sign an acknowledgment form confirming they have received,
read, and understood this handbook.

This handbook is not a contract of employment. TechCorp reserves the right to
modify policies at any time with or without notice.

For questions about any policy, contact the Human Resources department at
hr@techcorp.com or extension 8500.
