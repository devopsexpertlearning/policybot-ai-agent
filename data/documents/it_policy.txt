1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
================================================================================

This IT Policy establishes guidelines for the appropriate use of TechCorp's
information technology resources, including computers, networks, software,
data, and communication systems.

Scope: This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and
third parties with access to TechCorp IT resources.

================================================================================
2. ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY
================================================================================

2.1 Permitted Use
IT resources are provided for business purposes. Limited personal use is
permitted if it:
- Does not interfere with work responsibilities
- Does not consume significant resources
- Complies with all company policies
- Does not violate any laws or regulations

2.2 Prohibited Activities
The following activities are strictly prohibited:
- Accessing, storing, or transmitting illegal content
- Unauthorized access to systems or data
- Installing unauthorized software
- Sharing login credentials
- Using IT resources for commercial gain outside of job duties
- Circumventing security measures
- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile communications
- Downloading or distributing pirated software or media

================================================================================
3. SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

3.1 Physical Security
- Lock workstations when leaving your desk (Windows + L)
- Never leave devices unattended in public places
- Secure laptops in locked drawers or use cable locks
- Immediately report lost or stolen devices

3.2 Software Security
- Keep all software up-to-date with security patches
- Use only approved, licensed software
- Run antivirus scans weekly (automatic scans are configured)
- Do not disable security software

3.3 Network Security
- Connect only to approved networks
- Use VPN for all remote connections
- Do not create unauthorized wireless networks
- Report suspicious network activity

================================================================================
4. DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY
================================================================================

4.1 Data Classification
TechCorp classifies data into three categories:

PUBLIC: Information intended for public disclosure
- Marketing materials
- Published reports
- Public website content

INTERNAL: Information for internal use only
- Internal memos
- General business documents
- Non-sensitive communications

CONFIDENTIAL: Sensitive information requiring protection
- Customer data
- Financial information
- Employee records
- Proprietary business information
- Source code and intellectual property

4.2 Data Handling Requirements

Confidential Data must be:
- Encrypted when stored or transmitted
- Accessed only by authorized personnel
- Not shared with unauthorized parties
- Properly disposed of (shredding, secure deletion)
- Protected with access controls

4.3 Personal Data
Comply with all privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) when handling personal
information. Collect, process, and store personal data only as necessary for
business purposes.

4.4 Data Retention
- Follow data retention schedules by data type
- Delete data when no longer needed
- Archive important information according to policy
- Never delete data subject to legal hold

================================================================================
5. PASSWORD MANAGEMENT
================================================================================

5.1 Password Requirements
All passwords must meet these minimum requirements:
- At least 12 characters long
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include numbers
- Include special characters
- No dictionary words or common patterns
- No personal information (names, birthdays, etc.)

5.2 Password Practices
- Change passwords every 90 days
- Never reuse previous passwords
- Use unique passwords for each system
- Never share passwords with anyone
- Do not write passwords down or store in plain text
- Use the company password manager for secure storage

5.3 Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
MFA is required for:
- Email accounts
- VPN access
- Cloud services
- Administrative accounts
- All external-facing systems

================================================================================
6. EMAIL AND COMMUNICATION
================================================================================

6.1 Email Usage
- Use professional language in all communications
- Include clear subject lines
- Do not send confidential information without encryption
- Be cautious with attachments from unknown senders
- Check recipient addresses before sending

6.2 Phishing Awareness
Be alert for phishing attempts:
- Unexpected requests for credentials or personal information
- Suspicious links or attachments
- Urgent or threatening language
- Sender addresses that look suspicious
If you suspect phishing, report to IT immediately: security@techcorp.com

6.3 Email Retention
- Email is company property and may be monitored
- Important emails should be saved to appropriate folders
- Delete unnecessary emails regularly
- Do not use personal email for company business

================================================================================
7. INTERNET USAGE
================================================================================

7.1 Appropriate Use
Internet access is provided for business purposes. Reasonable personal use
is permitted during breaks.

7.2 Prohibited Content
Do not access websites containing:
- Illegal content
- Pornography or adult material
- Gambling
- Hate speech
- Pirated software or media
- Malware or hacking tools

7.3 Social Media
- Be professional when representing the company online
- Do not share confidential information
- Clarify that personal opinions are your own
- Follow the social media policy in the Employee Handbook

7.4 Downloads
- Download files only from trusted sources
- Scan all downloads with antivirus software
- Do not download executable files from unknown sources
- Request IT approval for new software installations

================================================================================
8. MOBILE DEVICES
================================================================================

8.1 Company-Owned Devices
- Use provided mobile device management (MDM) software
- Keep devices updated with latest OS and security patches
- Enable device encryption
- Set up device lock with PIN/biometric authentication
- Report lost or stolen devices immediately

8.2 Bring Your Own Device (BYOD)
If approved for BYOD:
- Install required MDM software
- Separate work and personal data
- Allow remote wipe capability for company data
- Use strong device security
- Follow all security policies

8.3 App Installation
- Only install approved business apps
- Download apps only from official app stores
- Review app permissions before installing
- Regularly update all apps

================================================================================
9. REMOTE ACCESS
================================================================================

9.1 VPN Requirements
When working remotely:
- Always connect via company VPN
- Use only approved VPN clients
- Never share VPN credentials
- Disconnect VPN when not in use

9.2 Remote Work Security
- Ensure home network is secured with WPA2/WPA3 encryption
- Use a private workspace away from public view
- Lock your device when stepping away
- Do not allow others to use company devices
- Maintain confidentiality during video calls

9.3 Public Wi-Fi
- Avoid public Wi-Fi when possible
- Always use VPN on public networks
- Never access confidential information on public Wi-Fi
- Disable automatic Wi-Fi connection

================================================================================
10. INCIDENT REPORTING
================================================================================

10.1 Security Incidents
Report immediately:
- Lost or stolen devices
- Suspected malware or virus infections
- Phishing attempts
- Unauthorized access attempts
- Data breaches or suspected breaches
- Unusual system behavior

10.2 Reporting Procedures
Contact IT Security:
- Email: security@techcorp.com
- Phone: x8888 (internal) or 555-0199 (external)
- Available 24/7 for urgent security issues

Do not attempt to resolve security incidents yourself.

10.3 Response Time
- Critical incidents: Immediate response
- High priority: Within 2 hours
- Medium priority: Within 4 hours
- Low priority: Within 24 hours

================================================================================11. COMPLIANCE AND VIOLATIONS
================================================================================

11.1 Monitoring
TechCorp reserves the right to monitor all use of IT resources, including:
- Email communications
- Internet browsing
- File access
- System logs
- Network traffic

There is no expectation of privacy when using company IT resources.

11.2 Compliance Audits
Regular audits are conducted to ensure policy compliance. Employees must
cooperate fully with audit requests.

11.3 Violations
Policy violations may result in:
- Warning
- Mandatory training
- Suspension of IT privileges
- Disciplinary action up to and including termination
- Legal action for serious violations

11.4 Reporting Violations
Report policy violations to:
- Your manager
- IT Department
- Human Resources
- Ethics Hotline: 1-800-555-0123
