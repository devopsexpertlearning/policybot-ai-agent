LEAVE POLICY
TechCorp Solutions
Effective Date: January 2026

================================================================================
1. INTRODUCTION
================================================================================

This policy outlines the various types of leave available to TechCorp employees.
All employees should familiarize themselves with these policies and consult with
Human Resources if they have questions.

================================================================================
2. PAID TIME OFF (PTO)
================================================================================

2.1 PTO Accrual
TechCorp provides Paid Time Off (PTO) that can be used for vacation, personal
time, or any other purpose at the employee's discretion.

Accrual Rates (based on years of service):
- Years 1-3: 15 days per year (1.25 days per month)
- Years 4-7: 20 days per year (1.67 days per month)
- Years 8+: 25 days per year (2.08 days per month)

2.2 PTO Usage
- PTO begins accruing from the first day of employment
- New employees can use accrued PTO after 90 days
- PTO must be requested and approved in advance when possible
- Requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- Manager approval required for all PTO requests

2.3 PTO Scheduling
- Blackout periods may apply during critical business periods
- Holiday periods (Thanksgiving, Christmas) fill quickly - plan ahead
- At least 50% of team must be available during business hours
- Extended leave (5+ consecutive days) requires manager approval

2.4 Maximum Accrual
- Maximum accrual cap: 1.5x annual allocation
- Once cap is reached, no additional PTO accrues until balance drops
- Use-it-or-lose-it: PTO over the cap is forfeited
- HR will send reminders when approaching cap

2.5 PTO Payout
- Unused PTO (up to 10 days) is paid out upon termination
- PTO above 10 days is forfeited
- Payout is at employee's current base pay rate
- PTO is not paid out for employees terminated for cause

================================================================================
3. SICK LEAVE
================================================================================

3.1 Sick Leave Allocation
- All full-time employees receive 10 days of sick leave per year
- Sick leave is separate from PTO
- Sick leave is granted in full on January 1st each year
- New hires receive prorated sick leave

3.2 Acceptable Uses
Sick leave may be used for:
- Personal illness or injury
- Medical, dental, or optical appointments
- Caring for an ill immediate family member (spouse, child, parent)
- Preventive medical care

3.3 Notification Requirements
- Notify manager as soon as possible, preferably before start of shift
- For absences of 3+ consecutive days, notification to HR required
- Doctor's note required for absences of 5+ consecutive days

3.4 Sick Leave Carryover
- Unused sick leave does not carry over to the next year
- Sick leave is not paid out upon termination
- Use-it-or-lose-it policy applies

================================================================================
4. PARENTAL LEAVE
================================================================================

4.1 Eligibility
Employees who have been with TechCorp for at least 12 months are eligible
for paid parental leave.

4.2 Leave Duration

Primary Caregiver:
- 12 weeks of paid leave
- May be taken continuously or intermittently within first year
- Full salary continuation during leave

Secondary Caregiver:
- 4 weeks of paid leave
- May be taken continuously or intermittently within first year
- Full salary continuation during leave

4.3 Qualifying Events
Parental leave may be taken for:
- Birth of a child
- Adoption of a child
- Foster care placement

4.4 Requesting Parental Leave
- Notify HR and manager at least 30 days before intended start date
- Complete parental leave request form
- Provide documentation (birth certificate, adoption papers, etc.)
- Coordinate transition plan with manager

4.5 Return to Work
- Guaranteed same or equivalent position upon return
- Benefits continue during leave period
- May extend with unpaid FMLA leave if eligible

================================================================================
5. FAMILY AND MEDICAL LEAVE (FMLA)
================================================================================

5.1 Eligibility
Employees eligible for FMLA if they:
- Have worked for TechCorp for at least 12 months
- Have worked at least 1,250 hours in the past 12 months

5.2 Reasons for FMLA Leave
- Birth and care of newborn child
- Adoption or foster care placement
- Care for immediate family member with serious health condition
- Employee's own serious health condition
- Qualifying military family leave

5.3 Leave Duration
- Up to 12 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave per year
- Up to 26 weeks for military caregiver leave
- May run concurrently with paid parental or sick leave

5.4 Benefits During FMLA
- Health insurance premiums continue (employee pays portion)
- Job protection guaranteed
- Accrual of seniority and benefits continues

5.5 Requesting FMLA Leave
- Submit request to HR at least 30 days in advance when possible
- Complete FMLA request forms
- Provide medical certification if applicable
- Recertification may be required for extended leave

================================================================================
6. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
================================================================================

6.1 Immediate Family
- 5 days of paid bereavement leave for death of immediate family member
- Immediate family: spouse, child, parent, sibling

6.2 Extended Family
- 3 days of paid bereavement leave for death of extended family member
- Extended family: grandparent, grandchild, in-law, aunt, uncle

6.3 Additional Leave
- Additional unpaid leave may be requested through manager and HR
- PTO may be used to extend bereavement leave

6.4 Documentation
- Death certificate or obituary may be required
- Submit bereavement leave request to HR

================================================================================
7. JURY DUTY AND COURT LEAVE
================================================================================

7.1 Jury Duty
- Employees summoned for jury duty receive paid time off
- Provide jury summons to manager and HR as soon as received
- Any jury pay received offsets company pay
- Return to work if excused early from jury duty

7.2 Court Appearances
- Paid leave for court appearances when employee is:
  * Subpoenaed as a witness
  * Required to testify
  * Attending adoption or custody hearings
- Unpaid leave for personal lawsuits or criminal proceedings

================================================================================
8. MILITARY LEAVE
================================================================================

8.1 Eligibility
All employees who serve in U.S. uniformed services are eligible for military
leave in accordance with USERRA.

8.2 Leave Duration
- Up to 5 years cumulative for military service
- Additional time for training, deployments, or service obligations

8.3 Pay During Leave
- First 10 days: Full pay
- Days 11+: Unpaid (may use PTO)
- Benefits continue according to USERRA requirements

8.4 Notification
- Provide military orders to HR as soon as received
- Verbal notice acceptable for short-notice deployments
- Written documentation required when available

8.5 Reinstatement Rights
- Guaranteed reinstatement to same or equivalent position
- Apply for reinstatement within specified timeframe
- Seniority and benefits protection per USERRA

================================================================================
9. VOTING LEAVE
================================================================================

9.1 Time Off to Vote
- Employees are encouraged to vote during non-work hours
- If work schedule prevents voting, up to 2 hours paid leave  provided
- Must request in advance
- May be required to provide proof of voting

================================================================================
10. EDUCATIONAL LEAVE
================================================================================

10.1 Continuing Education
- Unpaid educational leave may be granted for approved programs
- Must be work-related or career development
- Requires manager and HR approval
- Maximum 1 year leave

10.2 Tuition Reimbursement
- See Professional Development policy
- Available for approved courses and degree programs
- Up to $5,000 per year

================================================================================
11. PERSONAL LEAVE OF ABSENCE
================================================================================

11.1 Eligibility
Employees with 2+ years of service may request unpaid personal leave for:
- Extended travel
- Personal matters
- Family obligations
- Other compelling reasons

11.2 Duration
- Maximum 90 days in a calendar year
- Manager and HR approval required
- Benefits may be affected - consult HR

11.3 Return to Work
- No job guarantee (depends on availability)
- Must give 30 days notice of intended return
- May need to reapply for positions

================================================================================
12. SABBATICAL LEAVE
================================================================================

12.1 Eligibility
Employees with 7+ years of continuous service are eligible for sabbatical.

12.2 Sabbatical Terms
- Up to 8 weeks of paid leave
- Available once every 7 years
- Must be used for rest, travel, or personal development
- Requires 6 months advance notice

12.3 Approval Process
- Submit sabbatical proposal to manager and HR
- Must include transition plan
- Company reserves right to postpone for business needs
- Limited number of sabbaticals approved per quarter

================================================================================
13. LEAVE REQUEST PROCEDURES
================================================================================

13.1 How to Request Leave
1. Discuss with manager
2. Submit leave request in HR system
3. Provide required documentation
4. Await approval
5. Plan transition/coverage

13.2 Emergency Leave
- Contact manager immediately
- Follow up with formal request within 24 hours
- Provide documentation as soon as possible

13.3 Leave Tracking
- All leave is tracked in the HR system
- Employees can view balances online
- Questions about balances: contact HR

================================================================================
14. GENERAL PROVISIONS
================================================================================

14.1 Part-Time Employees
Part-time employees receive prorated leave based on scheduled hours.

14.2 Benefits During Leave
- Health insurance: Continued for paid leave periods
- PTO accrual: Continues during paid leave, stops during unpaid leave
- Other benefits: Varies by leave type - consult HR

14.3 Failure to Return
- Failure to return from leave may be considered voluntary resignation
- Final paycheck includes unused PTO (per payout policy)
- Company property must be returned

14.4 Policy Compliance
- Falsifying leave requests is grounds for termination
- Leave must be used for stated purpose
- Working during leave may result in loss of benefits
