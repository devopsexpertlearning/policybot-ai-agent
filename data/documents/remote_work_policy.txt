REMOTE WORK AND FLEXIBLE WORK ARRANGEMENTS POLICY
TechCorp Solutions
Effective Date: January 2026

1. POLICY OVERVIEW

TechCorp Solutions recognizes that flexible work arrangements can enhance productivity, work-life balance, and employee satisfaction. This policy outlines guidelines for remote work, hybrid schedules, and other flexible arrangements.

1.1 Guiding Principles
- Trust and accountability are foundational
- Flexibility should not compromise collaboration or productivity
- Arrangements must meet both employee and business needs
- Equity and fairness across teams
- Regular evaluation and adjustment

2. WORK ARRANGEMENT OPTIONS

2.1 Fully Remote
- Work from home or approved remote location full-time
- No regular office presence required
- Must live within 2 hours of company time zone
- Available for roles that can be performed entirely remotely

2.2 Hybrid
- Combination of office and remote work
- Typical schedules:
  * 3 days office / 2 days remote
  * 2 days office / 3 days remote
- Team determines core collaboration days
- Flexibility for personal appointments

2.3 Flexible Hours
- Adjust start/end times within core hours
- Core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM (must be available)
- Early birds: 7 AM - 3 PM
- Night owls: 10 AM - 6 PM
- Must maintain 40-hour work week

2.4 Compressed Work Week
- Work full-time hours in fewer than 5 days
- Example: 4 days x 10 hours
- Requires manager approval
- Must cover all business needs

3. ELIGIBILITY AND APPROVAL

3.1 General Eligibility
- Completed probationary period (90 days)
- Demonstrated strong performance
- Role can be performed remotely
- Reliable home internet connection
- Appropriate workspace

3.2 Role Suitability
Highly suitable:
- Software engineering
- Data science
- Product management
- Design
- Customer support (with proper setup)

Limited suitability:
- Roles requiring physical presence
- Lab work or hardware testing
- Facilities management
- Reception and office services

3.3 Approval Process
1. Employee submits request to manager
2. Manager evaluates based on:
   - Role requirements
   - Team needs
   - Performance history
   - Business impact
3. Manager approves or discusses alternatives
4. HR documents arrangement
5. 30-day trial period
6. Regular reviews (quarterly)

4. REMOTE WORK REQUIREMENTS

4.1 Technology Requirements
Employee must have:
- Reliable high-speed internet (minimum 25 Mbps download, 5 Mbps upload)
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Ergonomic setup (desk, chair)
- Quiet environment for video calls

Company provides:
- Laptop and necessary peripherals
- VPN access
- Collaboration tools (Slack, Zoom, etc.)
- Monitor, keyboard, mouse (upon request)
- Stipend for home office setup: $500 one-time

4.2 Security Requirements
- Use company-provided VPN for all work
- Secure home Wi-Fi network (WPA2 or better)
- No public Wi-Fi for sensitive work
- Lock screen when away from desk
- Comply with all IT security policies
- No family members using work devices

4.3 Workspace Safety
- Ergonomic workspace setup
- Adequate lighting
- Proper ventilation
- Free from hazards
- Complies with local building codes

5. EXPECTATIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES

5.1 Availability and Communication
- Be online and responsive during agreed work hours
- Respond to messages within 2 hours during work hours
- Update calendar with availability
- Attend all required meetings
- Use video for team meetings
- Proactive communication about status and blockers

5.2 Productivity and Deliverables
- Meet all deadlines and commitments
- Maintain same productivity as in-office work
- Participate actively in team activities
- Complete all assigned work
- Track time accurately

5.3 Collaboration
- Attend in-person meetings when required
- Participate in team events and offsites
- Be available for impromptu discussions
- Contribute to team culture
- Mentor and support colleagues

6. HYBRID WORK GUIDELINES

6.1 Office Days
- Teams designate core collaboration days
- Typically Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- All team members in office on core days
- Advance notice required for changes
- Book desk through office management system

6.2 Remote Days
- Monday and Friday typically remote
- Focus time for deep work
- Fewer meetings scheduled
- Asynchronous communication preferred

6.3 Desk Hoteling
- No assigned desks for hybrid workers
- Reserve desk through booking system
- Clean desk policy - take belongings daily
- Lockers available for personal items

7. INTERNATIONAL AND OUT-OF-STATE REMOTE WORK

7.1 Domestic Remote Work (Different State)
- Requires VP approval
- Tax and legal implications reviewed
- Must establish business presence in new state
- Employee responsible for state tax filing
- May affect compensation (based on location)

7.2 International Remote Work
- Temporary (up to 30 days): Manager approval
- Extended (30-90 days): VP approval
- Permanent: Not currently supported
- Considerations:
  * Time zone differences (max 3 hours from PT)
  * Tax implications
  * Work authorization
  * Data privacy laws

7.3 "Workcation" Policy
- Work remotely while traveling
- Up to 2 weeks per quarter
- Must maintain normal work hours
- Reliable internet required
- Manager approval needed
- Not a substitute for vacation

8. EQUIPMENT AND EXPENSES

8.1 Company-Provided Equipment
- Laptop (refreshed every 3 years)
- Monitor (upon request)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset for calls
- Webcam (if laptop camera insufficient)
- Docking station (if needed)

8.2 Home Office Stipend
- $500 one-time setup stipend
- $50/month ongoing for internet and utilities
- Reimbursed via expense report
- Taxable benefit

8.3 Ergonomic Equipment
- Ergonomic chair: Up to $400
- Standing desk converter: Up to $300
- Monitor arm: Up to $100
- Requires manager approval
- Must submit receipt for reimbursement

8.4 Equipment Return
- All company equipment must be returned upon:
  * Termination of employment
  * Change to in-office arrangement
  * Equipment upgrade
- Failure to return may result in paycheck deduction

9. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

9.1 Performance Expectations
- Same standards as in-office employees
- Measured by results, not hours
- Regular check-ins with manager
- Clear goals and OKRs
- Documented in performance reviews

9.2 Monitoring and Productivity
- No invasive monitoring software
- Trust-based system
- Managers track deliverables and outcomes
- Regular 1-on-1s to discuss progress
- Performance issues addressed promptly

9.3 Modification or Termination of Arrangement
Remote/flexible arrangements may be modified or terminated if:
- Performance declines
- Business needs change
- Communication issues arise
- Technology problems persist
- Team collaboration suffers

Notice period:
- 30 days for performance-related changes
- 60 days for business-need changes
- Immediate for policy violations

10. TEAM COLLABORATION AND CULTURE

10.1 Team Meetings
- All-hands: Monthly (in-person or hybrid)
- Team meetings: Weekly (video required)
- 1-on-1s: Bi-weekly minimum
- Sprint planning/retros: As needed

10.2 In-Person Requirements
- Quarterly team offsites (2-3 days)
- Annual company summit (1 week)
- Department meetings (monthly for hybrid)
- New hire onboarding (first 2 weeks in-office)
- Travel expenses covered by company

10.3 Building Connections
- Virtual coffee chats encouraged
- Slack channels for social connection
- Remote-friendly team building activities
- Inclusive meeting practices (everyone on video)

11. MANAGER RESPONSIBILITIES

11.1 Supporting Remote Teams
Managers must:
- Set clear expectations and goals
- Communicate regularly and clearly
- Provide necessary tools and resources
- Trust employees to manage their time
- Address issues promptly
- Ensure equity between remote and in-office employees
- Model good remote work practices

11.2 Inclusive Leadership
- Don't default to in-office employees for opportunities
- Ensure remote employees are included in decisions
- Rotate meeting times to accommodate different schedules
- Recognize remote employees' contributions
- Provide equal access to development opportunities

12. HEALTH AND WELLNESS

12.1 Work-Life Balance
- Respect work hours boundaries
- No expectation to respond outside work hours
- Encourage breaks and time off
- Discourage always-on culture
- Support mental health and wellness

12.2 Ergonomics and Health
- Ergonomic assessment available (virtual)
- Encourage movement and breaks
- Standing desk options
- Wellness stipend applies to home office

12.3 Preventing Isolation
- Regular team check-ins
- Encourage social connections
- Mental health resources available
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

13. COMPLIANCE AND LEGAL

13.1 Workers' Compensation
- Home office injuries may be covered
- Report all work-related injuries immediately
- Same process as office injuries
- Maintain safe workspace

13.2 Wage and Hour
- Non-exempt employees must track all hours
- Overtime must be pre-approved
- No working off the clock
- Meal and rest breaks required

13.3 Confidentiality and Data Security
- Same confidentiality obligations
- Secure handling of company data
- No discussing confidential matters where others can hear
- Shred sensitive documents
- Follow data retention policies

14. TEMPORARY REMOTE WORK

14.1 Occasional Remote Work
- All employees may work remotely occasionally
- Manager approval required
- Examples: home repair, delivery, minor illness
- Not a substitute for sick leave or PTO

14.2 Emergency Remote Work
- Natural disasters, pandemics, etc.
- Company may mandate remote work
- All employees expected to work remotely if able
- Flexibility for those with challenges (childcare, etc.)

15. POLICY REVIEW AND UPDATES

15.1 Regular Review
- Policy reviewed annually
- Updated based on feedback and business needs
- Changes communicated via email and all-hands

15.2 Feedback
- Employees encouraged to provide feedback
- Anonymous survey conducted quarterly
- Continuous improvement mindset

All employees with flexible work arrangements must:
- Read and understand this policy
- Sign acknowledgment form
- Complete remote work training
- Comply with all requirements

Failure to comply may result in:
- Modification of arrangement
- Return to office requirement
- Disciplinary action
